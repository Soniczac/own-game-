Doodle world is a fun pet collecting turn based pvp rpg game in the role of pokemon in this fun game you can collect trade and battle doodles. Your mother has signed you up for the island’s fun key hunt where you have to find 8 keys but is that really all there is to it. Along the way you’ll find that this key hunt isn’t what is says it is and it’s up to you to stop anyone who wants to use the keys for evil. With your friends find this evil and stop it before it’s too late.
